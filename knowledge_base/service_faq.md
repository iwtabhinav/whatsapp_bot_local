# Preimo Chauffeur Service FAQ

## Vehicle Types and Rates

### What types of vehicles do you offer?
We offer four types of luxury vehicles:
1. Sedan: Perfect for 1-3 passengers
   - Base rate: AED 120
   - Per kilometer: AED 3
2. SUV: Ideal for 4-6 passengers
   - Base rate: AED 180
   - Per kilometer: AED 4
3. Luxury (Maybach): Ultimate luxury experience
   - Base rate: AED 350
   - Per kilometer: AED 8
4. Van: Spacious option for up to 8 passengers
   - Base rate: AED 220
   - Per kilometer: AED 5

### What's included in the base rate?
- Professional chauffeur
- Vehicle cleaning and preparation
- Basic amenities (water, tissues)
- 15 minutes complimentary waiting time
- Air-conditioned comfort
- Insurance coverage

## Booking Policies

### How do I make a booking?
You can book through:
1. WhatsApp booking assistant
2. Our website
3. Phone call to our booking center

### What information do I need to provide?
- Your name
- Pickup location
- Drop-off location
- Pickup time
- Vehicle preference
- Any special requirements

### How far in advance should I book?
- Regular bookings: At least 2 hours notice
- Airport transfers: 4 hours notice recommended
- Special events: 24 hours notice preferred

## Payment Information

### What payment methods do you accept?
- Credit/Debit cards
- Bank transfer
- Cash (for regular customers only)
- Digital wallets

### When do I need to pay?
Payment is required at the time of booking confirmation. The booking is only confirmed after successful payment.

### What is your refund policy?
- Full refund: Cancellation 24+ hours before pickup
- 50% refund: Cancellation 12-24 hours before pickup
- No refund: Cancellation less than 12 hours before pickup
